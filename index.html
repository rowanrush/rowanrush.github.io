About Me

I am a PhD student in the School of Geosciences at the University of Edinburgh, supervised by Professor Tim Cresswell, Dr Nina Morris, and Kath Pierce.

I am a qualified archivist, having gained an MSc in Archives and Records Management from the University of Glasgow in 2020. Currently, I am working with the Centre for Research Collections at the University of Edinburgh, on the Regional Ethnology of Scotland (RESP) Project Within which I am contributing to the development of a geo-mapping discovery platform for the collection

Doctoral Research

In 2020 I commenced a doctoral project at the University of Edinburgh funded by the Scottish Graduate School for Arts and Humanities called 'Cultural Geographies of LGBTQ+ Entrepreneurship'. This research is a Collaborative Doctoral Award, with Somewhere Edi as a partner organisation and supervisor. My supervisors are Professor Tim Cresswell, Dr Nina Morris, and Kath Pierce.

My research interests span cultural geography, queer and transgender geographies and rurality.
