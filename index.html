About Me

I am a PhD student in the School of Geosciences at the University of Edinburgh.

In 2018 I was awarded an undergraduate degree in Geography from the University of Glasgow.

I am a qualified archivist after graduating from the Archives and Records Managemnt MSc at the University of Glasgow in 2020.

Learn More

Doctoral Research

In 2020 I commenced a doctoral project funded by the Scottish Graduate School for Arts and Humanities called 'Cultural Geographies of LGBTQ+ Entrepreneurship'. This research is a Collaborative Doctoral Award, with Somewhere Edi as a partner organisation and supervisor. My supervisors are Professor Tim Cresswell, Dr Nina Morris, and Kath Pierce.

My research interests span cultural geography, queer and transgender geographies, rurality, and island studies.
